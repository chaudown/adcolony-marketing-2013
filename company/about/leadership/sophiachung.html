Sophia Chung

VP Publisher Products

Sophia Chung is Vice President of Product at AdColony where she leads efforts to develop world-class SDKs and advertising technologies to enable supply/demand-side partner integrations. With over 15 years of experience in product management and development, Ms. Chung most recently worked at YP.com (formerly AT&T Interactive), spearheading the design and roadmap for its Hyperlocal mobile display ad products. She also played a key role in building the YP location-based ad network, which serves close to 1.8 billion monthly impressions to premium mobile publishers. Prior to YP, Ms. Chung was with a mobile search advertising startup leading the efforts to integrate real-time metadata APIs from 100+ content partners in various verticals as well as responsible for product design and launch of white-labeled mobile search solutions with partners and carriers in Asia, Europe and US. Ms. Chung started her career with Microsoft as a software engineer and later with Goldman Sachs in technology development.


Ms. Chung holds both an MS and BS in Computer& Electrical Engineering from Purdue University.
