Abe Pralle

VP Technology

Abe Pralle is Vice President of Technology at AdColony and manages the company's engineering organization and best practices with a particular focus on the development of the AdColony SDK. Prior to joining AdColony, Mr. Pralle taught Computer Science at Northern Arizona University where he specialized in compilers, API design, and cross-platform infrastructures. He is the author of the open-source Plasmacore cross-platform game programming framework.


Mr. Pralle holds a MS in Computer Science and Engineering from Northern Arizona University.
