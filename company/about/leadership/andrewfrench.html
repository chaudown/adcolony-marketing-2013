Andrew French

General Manager EMEA

Andrew French is AdColony’s GM of EMEA based out of London, England where he leads AdColony’s growing global business by providing support and strategic account management for top publishers and advertisers in the region.

Prior to joining AdColony, Andrew was VP Client Services on the media team at Somo, one of the largest independent mobile solutions companies in the world, where he was responsible for all media revenue. Andrew was Somo’s second ever employee and built out the company’s international client list as head of sales, growing the business from zero to over forty clients including Audi, Burberry, Groupon, Zynga and Dominos Pizza in their first two years. While at Somo he also set up the Global Trading division to ensure clients received world-class performance from their media campaigns, working with over 250 partners to ensure the right media was selected for the right campaigns, all delivered using best-in-class tools and technology.

Prior to Somo, Andrew was with mobile marketing company AdIQ where he helped brands take their first steps into mobile, building sites, SMS campaigns and some of the first ever apps across all sectors for clients such as including Sony Ericsson, The Army, LG and Barclays.

Andrew is UK Advisory Board Director for the Mobile Marketing Association (MMA) and attended Loughborough University. He is a frequent speaker at mobile industry events such as Mobile World Congress, Cross Media, ICE, AdWeek and more. He can be reached at andrew.french@adcolony.com.
