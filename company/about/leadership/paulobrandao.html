Paulo Brandao

VP Product

Paulo Brandao is Vice President of Product and Innovation at AdColony and oversees new product development. Mr. Brandao has been directly involved in the design and production of the AdColony mobile video ad network as well as other mobile games and applications across multiple platforms. A hobby technologist, childhood PC-gamer, and developer, he has extensive knowledge of the web, mobile, advertising, social media and game development.


Mr. Brandao holds a BA in Political Science from the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor.
