Ty Heath

Chief Technology Officer

Ty Heath is Chief Technology Officer of AdColony and oversees the technical direction of the entire AdColony system. This includes architecture, infrastructure, product pipeline, feature design, development, and testing of one of the world's leading mobile video advertising platforms. Prior to joining AdColony, Mr. Heath worked at Peer Media Technologies, designing and executing much of the company's massively networked anti-piracy and file sharing intelligence infrastructure.


Mr. Heath holds a joint BS in both Computer Science and Electrical Engineering degree from Northern Arizona University.
