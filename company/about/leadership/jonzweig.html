Jon Zweig

Founder & President

Jonathan Zweig is Founder and President of AdColony, the leader in breakthrough mobile video advertising. With deep roots in mobile app development, Mr. Zweig has been featured in Forbes and the Wall Street Journal for his work at the company. Prior to AdColony, Mr. Zweig designed and implemented medical software for massive patient data sets at UCLA Radiology.


Mr. Zweig holds a BS in Computer Science from the University of California, Los Angeles.
