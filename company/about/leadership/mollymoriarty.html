Molly Moriarty

VP Marketing

Molly (Heintz) Moriarty is Vice President of Marketing at AdColony where she oversees the company’s marketing strategy and executions across events, public relations, research, advertising and other initiatives to promote AdColony’s solutions for advertisers and publishers throughout the mobile landscape. Prior to joining AdColony, Molly was Director of Communications at digital advertising company SocialVibe where she led all aspects of marketing communications and media relations. Before joining SocialVibe, Molly worked on the in-house marketing team at FOX Broadcasting Company where she did publicity and integrated marketing for primetime television shows including American Idol, House, The Simpsons and others.


Molly earned her BA at Yale University and holds an MA in Communication Management from the USC Annenberg School for Communication & Journalism.
