Reia Davidson

Sales Partnership Manager

Reia Davidson is Sales Partnership Manager at AdColony, leading the company’s strategic partnerships globally. Prior to joining AdColony, Ms. Davidson built and managed sales teams as Revolution Prep’s West Coast Sales Director, orchestrating the start-up’s nationwide rollout and helping it grow into the largest provider of test preparation services in the country. Ms. Davidson began her career as a Center Director for SCORE! Educational Centers and has also held positions in business development and sales partnerships at ESN Interactive and in engagement marketing at Toyota Motor Sales, USA.


Ms. Davidson holds an MBA with honors from the USC Marshall School of Business and a BA in Communications from the University of California, Los Angeles, where she graduated magna cum laude.
