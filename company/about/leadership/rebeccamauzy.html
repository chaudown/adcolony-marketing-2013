Rebecca Mauzy

VP Product Management

Rebecca Mauzy is Vice President of Product Management at AdColony, leading the effort to develop and implement cutting-edge technologies and features into the core AdColony product. Ms. Mauzy has previously held positions at Putnam Investments and Boston College, where she started cultivating her love for technology and product development. She was most recently a Director of Mobile Advertising Operations at AdColony, where she developed a deep knowledge of the AdColony product and mobile advertising systems and technologies.


Ms. Mauzy holds BS in Operations & Strategic Management and Finance from Boston College in Boston, Massachusetts.
